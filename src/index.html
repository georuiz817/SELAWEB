About SELA

Siargao Experiential Learning Academy(SELA) is a school founded in November of 2024 out of a need for an effective alternative learning in the island. Ever since, SELA has been successful in giving the high quality education through experiences which were achieved in 2 different ways:

First, students go through high quality experiences because they are being guided by EXPERTS IN THEIR FIELDS. We have external teachers / partners that can guide students in different fields that promotes practical skills, competitiveness and discipline which can be helpful in students overall development.

Second, we are a VERY EXCLUSIVE GROUP with only 10 students. Having said that, we are able to cater to each students needs and; academic and behavioral goals that are set by both the parents and the school upon enrollment; and after the first week after observation to make sure that the goals are realistic as to not put pressure on the students.
